About Om Tailors

With over 17 years of experience in creating exceptional custom clothing.

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

At Om Tailors, we take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to quality. With more than 17 years of experience, each garment is carefully crafted to meet our clients' exact specifications and ensure a perfect fit.

Our master tailors combine traditional techniques with modern styling to create clothing that not only looks great but feels comfortable and lasts for years. We specialize in gents wear including shirts, pants, kurta pajamas, school dresses, job uniforms, and exclusive Lakhani shirts.

We work with a wide selection of premium fabrics from around the world, offering you countless options for creating your perfect wardrobe. Our alteration services including shirt pant raffu, fitting adjustments, and waist fitting are renowned for their precision.
